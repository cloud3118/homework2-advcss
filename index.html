Get To Know Me


Casey Loudermilk here. Dad, husband, and currently...full stack developer in training.

My story starts where most start...trying to figure out what I want to be when I grow up. At 36 years old, I have finally found something that frustrates me and excites me....coding. I have been involved in the business world for many years, but always felt like something was missing. I believe I am finally on that journey to fill in that gap.

I am a new father with a wonderful wife of 3 years. When I am not pulling all nighters at the computer or trying to wrap my brain around new development concepts, I love teaching my son new things, trying to outwit my wife with Seinfeld references (she usually wins), or spending time with my family. I was born and raised in the Atlanta area and have a passion for sports, music, and discussions over old-fashioneds.

So cheers, let's talk about what I can do for you over a drink.
